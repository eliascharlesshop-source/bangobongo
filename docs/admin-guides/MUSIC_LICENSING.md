# Music Licensing & Distribution System

This document outlines BangoBongo's complete music licensing, distribution, and e-commerce system integration.

## System Overview

### 1. BangoBongo Original Releases
- **Purpose**: Showcase, streaming, and global distribution
- **Distribution**: Managed through Ditto Music API
- **Rights**: BangoBongo retains full master rights
- **Availability**: Free streaming on BangoBongo + global distribution
- **Revenue**: Streaming royalties from Spotify, Apple Music, etc.

### 2. Licensed Beats/Tracks
- **Purpose**: Commercial licensing to customers
- **Distribution**: Direct sales through BangoBongo platform
- **Rights**: Various licensing tiers available
- **Availability**: Purchase-based licensing (3 tiers)

### 3. Physical Merchandise & Gear
- **Purpose**: Brand merchandise and music gear sales
- **Distribution**: Shopify-powered e-commerce
- **Integration**: Seamless cart and checkout experience
- **Fulfillment**: Shopify handles payment processing and shipping

## 3-Tier Licensing System (BeatStars Model)

### Basic Lease ($29)
- **Rights**: Non-exclusive
- **Streams**: Up to 2,500
- **Downloads**: Up to 2,500
- **Music Videos**: 1
- **Distribution**: No major platforms
- **Radio**: No broadcast rights
- **Credit**: Producer tag required
- **Term**: 2 years

### Premium Lease ($59) 🏆 Most Popular
- **Rights**: Non-exclusive
- **Streams**: Up to 10,000
- **Downloads**: Up to 10,000
- **Music Videos**: 2
- **Distribution**: Streaming platforms allowed
- **Radio**: Broadcast rights included
- **Credit**: Producer tag required
- **Term**: 3 years

### Unlimited Lease ($199)
- **Rights**: Non-exclusive
- **Streams**: Unlimited
- **Downloads**: Unlimited
- **Music Videos**: Unlimited
- **Distribution**: Full distribution rights
- **Radio & TV**: Broadcast rights included
- **Sync**: Sync licensing rights
- **Credit**: Producer tag required
- **Term**: Lifetime

## Technical Implementation

### API Integrations
- ✅ **Ditto Music API**: Distribution and analytics
- ✅ **Shopify Storefront API**: Product catalog and cart
- ✅ **Shopify Admin API**: Inventory and order management
- ✅ **Stripe API**: Payment processing for beats
- ✅ **Analytics APIs**: Comprehensive reporting

### Data Flow
1. **Track Upload** → Database + Ditto (if original)
2. **License Purchase** → Stripe → License Generation
3. **Merch Order** → Shopify → Payment → Fulfillment
4. **Analytics** → Ditto/Shopify → Unified Dashboard

### Security & Compliance
- **PCI Compliance**: Shopify handles payment security
- **GDPR/CCPA**: Customer data protection
- **Copyright Protection**: Content ID and takedowns
- **License Verification**: DRM and usage tracking
